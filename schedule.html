How to Use This Schedule

This schedule is a model to show how different types of support can be spread across the week: live sessions, self-paced study, and reflective time. In a real program, IMGs would choose the sessions that match their stage in the Match process, their time zone, and their personal responsibilities. The goal is to keep support consistent without overwhelming your calendar.
